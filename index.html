About Habiba's Help

We, the founders and members of Habiba's Help Charity, guided by compassion, justice, unity, and service to humanity, have established a non-political, non-profit, and non-discriminatory charitable organization. Our mission is to uplift the dignity of the Gambian people by addressing basic human needs, strengthening communities, empowering youth, and fostering sustainable development grounded in integrity, transparency, and collective responsibility.

Our group is inspired by the concern for poverty alleviation: poverty constitutes low income, low literacy, poor health and environmental degradation. The founders of the organization acknowledged the vitality of the individual and role of concerned groups in national development.

This innovation is premised on the prevailing national economic situation which affects the ability of the target group to meet their basic needs and efficiently support the education of their children. It is envisaged that the beneficiaries' quality of life will eventually be improved if they are given direct support based on their priorities and engaged in self-reliant economic activities of their choices for a reasonable length of time.

The founders are committed to working with the poor through Habiba's Help (H's H). The organization shall always recognize beneficiary action as evidence of interest and commitment. Habiba's Help is a non-governmental organization; a non-political, and a non-profit making Gambia-based charitable organization with the main thrust of poverty alleviation.
